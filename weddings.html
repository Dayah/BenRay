YOUR WEDDING DAY
Congratulations on your upcoming wedding! Your wedding photographs are the single most important investments you and your groom can make. No matter what, photographs will remian throughout your life together to remind you of the beginning of your life together. We at BenRay Photography know you will want to have beautiful memories of your wedding to cherish for many years to come. BenRay Photography specializes in preserving your day with distinctive and personalized portraits.
Wedding Announcement:
your protrait for the newspaper can be made with bride alone or the couple together. The Portraits need to be taken 4-6 weeks before the wedding.
Pre-pictures:
of the entire wedding party are taken 1½ hours before the wedding, including family, brothers, sisters, spouses, grandparents, their children, and any close friends. Please have everyone in the wedding party to dress early. Also tell the florist that the flowers are needed for the pictures.
Ceremony Pictures:
these pictures are usually held to a minimum due to the sacred nature of the ceremony. All Church rules will be observed. Any special requests or situations should be approved by the church administrator.
CONTRACT
  1. The photographer agrees to cover the whole ceremony and the reception. He will remain on hand until after the bride and groom depart.

  2. Pictures will be made of the ceremony with the consent of the church. If photographs can not be taken during the ceremonies, they will be restaged immediately after the services are completed.

  3. A deposit of $100.00 is required to secure the wedding date with the photographer and remaining balance is due on or before the wedding day.
